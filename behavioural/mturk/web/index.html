Welcome to our study!

This is a psychology experiment in which you will be shown pairs of images and will need to decide whether or not the images come from the same room. When the two images do come from the same room, their views will be pointing in a similar direction (see images below of floor plans and views that illustrate this point). The task will take about 1 minute to complete.

Before the task begins, there will be a brief training session. This training session will be identical to the actual experiment in every way, except that your responses will not be recorded and you will obtain feedback about the correct answers. The purpose of the training session is to give you a better understanding of the task.
